The Best Pizza Recipe

This is the best recipe for making pizza dough in a breadmaker. It is quick easy and requires few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 & 3/8 cup water
  • 2 tbl olive oil
  • 4 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix oil and water together. Water should be room temp.
  2. Add flour, yeast, and salt to water.
  3. Takes about an hour and half in the breadmaker.
